FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before starting an engagement.

How long does it take to build a custom AI solution?

Typical timeline: discovery (1 week) → prototype (2–3 weeks) → production deployment (1–2 weeks). A working solution is usually in your hands within 4–6 weeks of kickoff — faster for simpler use cases, longer for complex integrations.

What does the engagement process look like?

It starts with a complimentary 30-minute discovery call where you walk us through your workflows. From there: a short scoping phase to map priorities and quick wins, a roadmap you approve, a prototype validated on your real data, and then production rollout with training and documentation.

What kind of support do you provide after launch?

Every project is handed over with full documentation and team training. For ongoing monitoring, updates and new features, we offer monthly support packages — or you can run it independently. You own everything we build for you.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We regularly work with early-stage companies and structure engagements to fit their stage and budget. A focused pilot or proof of concept is often the most effective way to begin.

How do I know if AI will actually help my business?

That is precisely what the discovery call is for. In 30 minutes we will review your workflows and provide a candid assessment of where AI creates value — and where it does not. If it is not the right fit for your business, we will tell you.
